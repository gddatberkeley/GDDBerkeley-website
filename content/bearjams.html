Bear Jams

GDD hosts Bear Jams, a 45-hr game jam open to everyone, even non-UC Berkeley students!

Our full Rules and Guidelines can be found here: tinyurl.com/BearJams-Rules. Participants should follow the Code of Conduct: tinyurl.com/BearJams-CodeOfConduct.
